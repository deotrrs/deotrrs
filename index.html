Ideal Internship

My ideal internship is to have a team that work efficiently and also treat others like a professional. Here in our company, many employees said that "You can work here not just for money but you can work here with the family that the money can't be valued of" because, many developers just do work or do some programming but many of us as a Programmer feel that we don't have life in our field.

A team that treat us like a family that stays together and work together. In this type of team or group, you can be motivated to work with others, because, there are someone helping you, talk to you and motivates you to finish your task and also this type of team will make you to become a better employee.

Ideal Supervisor

My Ideal Supervisor is. He motivates his team, he can do work with the team, and mostly he loves his work and passionate on his position. Most supervisor of other companies were they just give you a task and you must do it on your own, mostly they just command you to do anything what they want or just leave you on your own work.

In my company it is the same supervisor that I wanted. He motivates us and also he also want to achieve goals for the team and also for the company. He makes us to be good in our team, sometimes when one of our colleague is giving-up on his work he just give some motivatation and he makes his/her to be a better person.
